About Edumantra

Edumantra provides effective management, coordination and administration of both the front and back end of the clients Learning Management Systems. It is a service that offers an ‘end-to-end solution’ for clients. Our training administration service cuts these costs and saves valuable resource. At Edumantra, we have helped out clients achieve high levels of excellence for many years.
